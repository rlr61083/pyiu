# Artemis II Crash Course

An applied introductory demo that introduces the fundamentals of Python and guides students through the process of creating their own visualization of the trajectory that the Orion module followed in its journey around the Moon during NASA's Artemis II mission. Learn more about this incredible mission and the people who made it possible [here](https://www.nasa.gov/artemis-ii-press-kit/).

Download **artemis_ii.ipynb** to get coding!
