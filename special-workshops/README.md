# PyIU Special Workshops

Outside of our regular scheduled events, PyIU instructors sometimes hold more specialized workshops for various student groups. This directory contains materials for those specialized workshops. Unlike the lesson materials available in the `crash-course` and `workshop` directories, the contents of these directories will not be actively maintained by the PyIU team.

- **palma-2026**: Materials for the PyIU workshops held for the IU Astronomy undergraduates participating in the Alice Palma Summer Research Program in the summer of 2026.
